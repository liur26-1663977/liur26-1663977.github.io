About Us

Dubshot Photography Club

Dubshot Photography Club is a UW Registered Student Organization dedicated to promote photography and learn from each other. We target professional photography but also provide education about photography to the public. We are the first UW Registered Student Organization that targets professional photography with the support of Bruce Hemingway, a Professor in Paul. G Allen School who teaches CSE 131, Introduction to Photography.

A fun fact about us is that EVERYONE in the executive board who have applied to CSE major are already inside the major which is quite impressive.

Our Slogan is to believe in profession.
