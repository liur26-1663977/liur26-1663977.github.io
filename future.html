Dubshot Photography Club

Workshop

A workshop is held regularly to promote photography, opens to everyone, without prior experiences required. Guest speakers in the field of photography and know the field of photography will be invited to speak about different aspects of photography. Hands on experiences may be provided to attendees.

Gallery

A gallery may be held once a year to show the original work of our members. This event helps promote photography and enables photographers share their work with others and learn from it.

Field Trip

A field trip is held to provide convenience to people who wants to explore the world and take photographs during the field trip. People can share their experience and exchange ideas to learn from the event.
